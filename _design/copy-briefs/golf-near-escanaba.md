**Body copy (DM Sans 400, three paragraphs):**

Nahma Golf Club is approximately 20 miles from downtown Escanaba — about a 20 to 25 minute drive heading west on US-2 to Ll Road in Rapid River. It is the closest public golf course with a lakeside setting in the region.

The course sits on the shore of Big Bay de Noc, the northern arm of Green Bay. Established in 1922, it is the second oldest golf course in Delta County. Nine holes, par 36, 2,875 yards. Public access, no tee time required, green fees starting at $18.

Escanaba golfers who have driven past the signs on US-2 and never stopped are the ones who are most surprised when they finally play. It is not what you expect from a course this close to the water.

The Nahma clubhouse is the right place for lunch before a round or a cold beer and pizza after the 9th hole. Full bar, sandwiches, pizza — open the same hours as the course. If you are looking for something to do in the Escanaba area that is not another trip to the waterfront or a drive downstate, this is the answer.

## Copy Rules Applied

- No em dashes. Commas or periods instead.
- Proximity leads the page (overline, H1, first paragraph).
- Lake is the primary differentiator — named in H1 subheadline, stat block, and closing CTA.
- "1922" appears in hero subheadline and stat block.
- Pricing ($18) appears in meta, stat block, and closing CTA.
- "Nahma Golf Club" on first mention. "Nahma" or "the course" after.
- No mention of club rentals, driving range, or dress code.
- Escanaba referenced by name throughout for SEO — this page targets that specific city.
