## Page Context

**Primary keywords:** upper peninsula golf courses, golf courses near escanaba mi, michigan golf courses  
**Audience:** Segment A (regional discovery golfer who does not know Nahma exists), Segment C (local golfer near Escanaba)  
**Intent:** Commercial investigation. The visitor is comparing courses and evaluating whether Nahma is worth the drive.  
**Conversion goal:** Click to Rates page or call for directions.  

**Messaging hierarchy (lead in this order):**  
1. The lakeside setting on Big Bay de Noc  
2. The 1922 founding (trust, history)  
3. Affordable public access ($18 starting price)  
4. The full-day clubhouse experience  
5. Leagues and community (supporting, not leading)

## Copy Rules Applied (Reference)

All copy in this brief follows the voice rules from `_seo/nahma-golf-club_info-and-strategy.md`:

- No em dashes used anywhere. Commas, periods, or parentheses instead.
- Lake leads in Section 1 (H1), Section 3, and Section 8.
- "1922" appears in the hero overline, trust bar, Section 3 body, and Section 4 stat.
- Pricing ($18) appears in the trust bar, Section 5, and meta description.
- "Nahma Golf Club" used on first page mention. "Nahma" or "the club" used after.
- No mention of club rentals, driving range, or reservations required.
- 18-hole play described explicitly as replaying the 9-hole course (Section 5 rate display structure handles this; rates page copy will clarify).
- Clubhouse experience (Section 6) leads with the social/experience angle, not specs.
