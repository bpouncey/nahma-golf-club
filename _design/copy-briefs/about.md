**H2 (Barlow Condensed Bold, uppercase):**

**Body copy (DM Sans 400, three paragraphs):**

Nahma Golf Club exists to provide affordable, accessible golf to the people of the Upper Peninsula. No dress code. No tee time requirement. No membership required to play. We believe golf should be available to anyone who wants to play it.

That belief is baked into our pricing, our leagues, and how we welcome everyone who walks through the door. For over a century, this course has been part of the fabric of Delta County. The founding goal was simple: a public course on the shore of Big Bay de Noc, open to the community.

Our goal is to keep it going for the next hundred years. We are a family-run club, not a resort operation. The people who play here are the community.

## Copy Rules Applied

- No em dashes. Commas or periods instead.
- "Nahma Golf Club" on first mention. "Nahma" or "the club" after.
- No em dashes in any of the three cards.
- The "What We're Not" card uses plain language — not snarky, just honest.
- "$18" appears in meta description, stat block, and card 3 body. Not overused.
- No mention of club rentals, driving range, or dress code requirements (beyond stating there isn't one).
