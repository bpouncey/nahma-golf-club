From Rapid River

Less Than 5 Miles
to Big Bay de Noc

Nahma Golf Club sits at 8588 Ll Road, just south of US-2 and less than 5 miles from downtown Rapid River. If you live in Rapid River or are passing through on US-2, the course is closer than most people realize. The turn is there and back before most golfers finish debating where to play.

The course is on the shore of Big Bay de Noc, the northern arm of Green Bay on Lake Michigan. It was established in 1922, making it one of the oldest public courses in the Upper Peninsula and the second oldest in Delta County. Nine holes, par 36, 2,875 yards. Public access, no tee time required, green fees from $18.

For Rapid River residents, Nahma is your home course. It has been here longer than most of the buildings on US-2, and it is still the only golf course in the area where you tee off with a view of the bay.

Directions

From Rapid River,
You're Already Close

From downtown Rapid River: head east on US-2 toward the Ll Road junction. Turn south onto Ll Road. Nahma Golf Club is at 8588 Ll Rd on your left, on the shore of Big Bay de Noc. The whole drive is under 10 minutes.

If you are coming off M-35 or US-2 from the west, the turn onto Ll Road comes before you reach Rapid River proper. Watch for the signs on US-2.

For Rapid River Regulars

A Day on the Bay,
Start to Finish

Rapid River sits at the edge of some of the best fishing and hunting territory in the Upper Peninsula, and the outdoor culture here runs deep. A round at Nahma fits the same rhythm. Show up when you want, pay $18, walk nine holes along the shore of Big Bay de Noc, and be back in Rapid River before lunch.

The Nahma clubhouse has a full bar, sandwiches, and pizza open the same hours as the course. Post-round drinks on the property, no need to drive back to town first. For Rapid River residents who want something more than a round here and there, three weekly leagues run throughout the season: Men's on Tuesdays at 6 PM, Scramble on Wednesdays at 6 PM, Women's on Thursdays at 6 PM.

Delta County does not have many courses, and none of the others are on the water. If you are from Rapid River and you have not played Nahma, the course has been here since 1922 waiting for you to find it. It is the closest public golf course to your front door, and it happens to be one of the best views in the county.
