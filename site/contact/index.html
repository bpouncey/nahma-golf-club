Getting Here

Nahma Golf Club is located in Michigan's Upper Peninsula, along the southern shoreline of Big Bay de Noc — one of the most scenic stretches of water on Lake Michigan.

We sit about 20 miles east of Escanaba on US-2, then north on county roads toward the bay. If you're coming from the Lower Peninsula, the Mackinac Bridge puts you roughly 90 miles east on US-2.
