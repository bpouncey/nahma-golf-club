The Clubhouse

After 9 holes on the shore of Big Bay de Noc, the round isn't over. It's just moving inside.

The Full Experience

Food, Drinks, and Good Company

The clubhouse is where the afternoon turns into an evening. Hot food, a full bar, and a relaxed atmosphere that fits the same people who play here: locals, visitors, groups, families.

This isn't a concession stand. It's a proper bar and kitchen, open every day the course is open, staffed and ready to serve.

For groups and outings, the clubhouse is what separates Nahma from a course that only has a cart shed. It's the reason groups come back.

Why It Matters

The 19th Hole on the Bay

There are courses in the Upper Peninsula where you finish your round and drive home. Nahma isn't that kind of place. Nine holes on the shoreline of Big Bay de Noc is already a half-day well spent. The clubhouse turns it into a full one.

Groups that book private outings at Nahma choose us in part because of what happens after the round. The scramble is over, the scores are settled, and everyone ends up at the bar with a cold drink and a hot slice. That's the experience groups come back for.
