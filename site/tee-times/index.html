How It Works

Show Up. Pay. Play.

Nahma Golf Club is a public course. No tee time reservation is required to play. During the season (May through September), you can arrive during regular hours, pay at the clubhouse, and head to the first tee.

Green fees start at $18 for nine holes. The course is walkable, and gas carts are available to rent if you prefer to ride.

Groups and Outings

Bringing a Group?

If you're planning to arrive with eight or more players, call ahead so we can accommodate your group properly. Nahma hosts private outings throughout the season, from corporate scrambles to family tournaments. The clubhouse has snacks, cold drinks, and a full bar for post-round gatherings.
