Green Fees

On the Course

Nine holes of golf on the shoreline of Big Bay de Noc, established in 1922. Show up and play. No membership required, no tee time needed.

UPGA (Upper Peninsula Golf Association) members receive a discounted rate. Membership with UPGA is separate from a Nahma Golf Club membership.
Walk-ins are always welcome. Large groups should call ahead.

Cart Rentals

Gas Cart Rentals

Gas carts are available for rent per round. Members receive a discounted cart rate. Trail fees apply for those who bring their own cart.

Personal carts require the annual trail fee to use the course.
Cart shed storage is available for the 12-month season.

Group Outings

Planning a Group Outing?

Private outings are available throughout the season. The clubhouse has a full bar, snacks, cold drinks, and space for your group after the round. Call or email to check availability and discuss pricing for large groups.
