From Gladstone

Half an Hour West
to Big Bay de Noc

Nahma Golf Club is approximately 25 miles from Gladstone, about a 25 to 30 minute drive heading west on US-2 through Rapid River and south on Ll Road. It is the only public lakeside golf course in the area, sitting directly on the shore of Big Bay de Noc.

Gladstone golfers already know Little Bay de Noc. Nahma puts you on the other bay, the bigger one, with a course that has been there since 1922. Nine holes, par 36, 2,875 yards. No tee time required, green fees starting at $18.

If you have been playing the same courses around Gladstone and want something different, Nahma is the most scenic option in Delta County and the one most people from Gladstone have not played.

Directions

How to Get Here
from Gladstone

From Gladstone, head north on US-2 (the same highway that runs through town) and continue west past Rapid River. Turn south onto Ll Road. Total drive is about 25 miles, with most of it on US-2 along the bay. Nahma Golf Club is at 8588 Ll Rd, Rapid River, MI 49878, on the left as you come down Ll Road toward the water.

  1. 01 From Gladstone, take US-2 west toward Rapid River. The road runs along the water for much of the drive.
  2. 02 Continue approximately 25 miles, passing through Rapid River. Stay on US-2 as it follows the shoreline.
  3. 03 Turn south onto Ll Road. Nahma Golf Club is on the left, on the shore of Big Bay de Noc.

For Gladstone Golfers

A Better View of the Bay

Gladstone sits on Little Bay de Noc. Nahma sits on Big Bay de Noc, the wider bay to the west. The drive between them is easy, mostly along US-2 with the water in view for stretches of the route. When you arrive, the course is right at the waterline. It is a different relationship with the bay than most golfers in the area have seen.

The Nahma clubhouse has a full bar, pizza, and sandwiches. It is the right place for a cold beer after nine holes or a full afternoon if you play 18 and sit on the back end of the round. If you are putting together a group from Gladstone, Nahma handles private outings throughout the season. Call ahead and the club can set it up.

Three weekly leagues run through the season: Men's on Tuesdays, an open All Scramble on Wednesdays, and Women's on Thursdays, all at 6 PM. Seasonal memberships start at $75 for students and $425 for seniors. Gladstone golfers who make the drive a few times tend to make it a habit.
