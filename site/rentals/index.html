Gas Cart Rental Rates

Ride the Course

Nahma Golf Club has gas carts available for rent on a first-come basis, but availability is limited. Show up, pay at the clubhouse, and you are out on the course. No advance booking needed.

Member pricing applies to UPGA members. Rates are per cart, not per person.

Round Regular Members

18-hole play means replaying the same 9-hole course. No carry-ons allowed in carts.

Important

Club Rentals Limited

Nahma Golf Club rents golf clubs on a first come, first serve basis. Players are encouraged to bring their own clubs.

Walk or Ride

Walking Is Always Welcome

The course is walkable. If you prefer to walk, you do not need a cart. Just pay your green fee at the clubhouse and head out.

Payment

Cards and Mobile Pay Accepted

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and NFC mobile payments. Cash is also accepted.
