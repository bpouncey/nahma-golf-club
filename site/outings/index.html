Why Nahma

A Lakeside Round with the Full Day Built In

Nahma Golf Club sits on the shoreline of Big Bay de Noc, on a course that has been welcoming groups since 1922. When your group finishes the round, the clubhouse is right there: snacks, cold drinks, and a full bar ready to go. No coordinating a separate restaurant reservation. No driving somewhere else. The whole outing, start to finish, happens in one place.

The course is flat, walkable, and forgiving enough for all skill levels. Whether your group is a mix of serious golfers and first-timers, everyone finishes the round. Green fees start at $18 per person, making Nahma one of the most affordable group outing destinations in the Upper Peninsula.

Large groups should call ahead. We will work with you on timing and logistics to make sure the day runs smoothly.

The Setting

Shoreline of Big Bay de Noc

Nine holes on the Lake Michigan shoreline. The bay is visible from the course. No other golf outing destination in the region offers this view.

The Clubhouse

Snacks, Cold Drinks, and a Full Bar

The clubhouse is the other half of the outing. Your group drinks, snacks, and settles the bets all in one place. No separate logistics. No extra cost to find.

The Course

Flat, Walkable, All Skill Levels

Par 36, 2,875 yards. Dish-shaped greens established in 1922. Easy to walk, enjoyable for every handicap. A scramble format works perfectly here.

The Value

Affordable for Groups

Green fees start at $18. Cart rentals available. No mandatory packages, no hidden fees. Your group pays for what they play.

Who Books with Us

Every Kind of Group Outing

01

Corporate Outings

A half-day on the bay is more memorable than a conference room. Teams finish the round, gather at the clubhouse, and leave with an experience worth talking about. Simple to organize, easy for everyone to participate in.

02

Fundraisers and Charity

Scramble formats work naturally on a 9-hole course. Outings can be organized as a single round or a two-round event. The course and clubhouse together make it easy to add a meal and a raffle without leaving the property.

03

Family Reunions

Golf for the golfers, the clubhouse for everyone else. A family outing at Nahma works because the setting is beautiful, the course is approachable, and the day doesn't end when the round does.

04

Friend Group Trips

A group of buddies on a Michigan golf trip needs a course that delivers on scenery and doesn't take itself too seriously. Nahma checks both. The bar being open after the round is not a small thing.

05

Annual Scrambles

Some groups come back every year. Named scrambles, trophy presentations, the same post-round table at the clubhouse. Nahma is the kind of course where that tradition makes sense.

Planning Your Outing

Three Steps to Book

01

Call to Discuss

Call us at (906) 644-2648. Tell us your group size, preferred date, and what you're looking for. We'll confirm availability and walk through the details. Large groups should call at least a few weeks ahead during peak season.

02

Set Your Format

Decide on your round format (scramble is the most popular for groups), how many holes, cart or walking, and whether you want the clubhouse reserved for a group meal afterward. We keep it flexible.

03

Show Up and Play

Your group arrives, tees off, and finishes at the clubhouse. Food, drinks, and the bar are ready when you come in. The course is open May through September, seven days a week.

After the Round
