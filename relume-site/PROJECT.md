## What This Site Is

Nahma Golf Club is a public 9-hole golf course on the shoreline of Big Bay de Noc (the northern arm of Lake Michigan's Green Bay), established in 1922. It is the second oldest golf course in Delta County, Michigan. Green fees start at $18. Walk-ins are always welcome. No tee time required.

The course is open May through September. It has 9 holes (par 36, 2,875 yards). 18-hole play means replaying the same 9-hole layout. There is a full clubhouse with food (snacks, sandwiches, pizza) and a full bar. Three weekly leagues run throughout the season. Private group outings are available.

**Key differentiators (in priority order):**
1. Lakeside setting on Big Bay de Noc — no competitor in the region has this
2. Established 1922 — second oldest course in Delta County
3. $18 green fees — affordable public access
4. Full clubhouse experience — food, full bar, post-round gathering
5. Active community calendar — three weekly leagues, seasonal scrambles, private outings

## Design Direction

**Aesthetic:** Classic, welcoming public golf club. Not a resort. Not a private club. Authentic and rooted.

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Forest green (#1B3A2D or similar deep green)
- Secondary: Cream/off-white (#F5F0E8 or similar)
- Accent: Sand/gold (#C9A84C or similar warm gold)
- Dark: Near-black for text (#1A1A1A)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Serif (think Playfair Display, Lora, or similar classic serif)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter, Source Sans, or similar)

**Photography:**
- Aerial/drone image of the course against Big Bay de Noc is the hero asset
- Bay views behind fairways
- Clubhouse interior and bar
- League nights and scramble action

**Tone:** Welcoming, confident, specific. Not promotional. Not flowery. Not resort marketing language. Michigan golfers are the audience — direct and honest copy.

## Content Rules (Apply to All Pages)

- **Always lead with the lake.** Big Bay de Noc belongs in the first sentence of every page.
- **Include "Established 1922" or "since 1922" in the first 100 words** of every primary page.
- **Never imply:** club rentals are available / a driving range exists / tee time reservations are required / the course is open year-round.
- **18-hole play:** Always describe as replaying the 9-hole course, never as a separate 18-hole layout.
- **No em dashes.** Use commas, periods, or parentheses instead.
- **Name usage:** "Nahma Golf Club" on first mention. "Nahma" or "the club" on subsequent mentions.
- **Pricing:** $18 green fee belongs in meta descriptions and above-the-fold copy wherever it fits naturally.
