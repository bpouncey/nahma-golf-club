### Intro Paragraph (centered, cream background)
Short informational paragraph above the scorecard table (80-100 words):
The scorecard shows yardages for two sets of tees. White tees are the standard men's tees. Red tees are the forward tees, used by many women players and newer golfers. HCP refers to the hole's handicap index — Hole 3 is the most difficult (HCP 1) and Hole 2 is the easiest (HCP 9). If you plan to play 18 holes, you will replay the same 9-hole course for the second nine. Add your scores together for an 18-hole total.
